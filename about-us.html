ABOUT US

Our mission is to provide people of all ages with the information and inspiration they need to make play an important part of their picnic day. On social media, people can find a way to play that can help build self-confidence, creativity, critical thinking, and other skills that will serve them throughout their lives. Plus, you might get ideas for how to keep playtime fun and fresh!
