Biscuit Sliding


INGREDIENTS:

• Biscuits (according to the number of people in the game)

PREP-TIME:

30 seconds for the players to answer.

LOCATION:

INDOOR

RECIPE FOR FUN

• Place a biscuit on the forehead of each player.
• The player has to slide the biscuit from their forehead to her mouth without the help of their hands.
• When a biscuit falls, the player is eliminated.

People Required to Play the Game

There can be any number of players.

How to Decide the Winner

The first person to bring the biscuit to her mouth is the winner.
