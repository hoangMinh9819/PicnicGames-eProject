1. Tourist camping in Buu Long

2. Cao Minh eco-tourism area

- Address: Hamlet 2, Vinh Tan Commune, Vinh Cuu, Dong Na
- Reference price: camping during the day: 35,000 VND / person - 70,000 VND / person overnight
- Can camp overnight

• Camping near Saigon will not be able to miss a beautiful place like Cao Minh tourist area. This place has ideal natural scenery that is suitable for you to explore and experience more.

3. Camp Ba Den Tay Ninh

- Address: Thanh Tan commune, Tay Ninh city, Tay Ninh province
- Reference price: adults 16,000 VND - children 8,000 VND
- Can camp overnight
• If you are a fanatic, immediately add Ba Den mountain to this list of when near Saigon camp! This place is known as the highest mountain in the South so many people often call it the name of the roof of the Southeast.
• To conquer the top of Ba Den mountain you can choose to travel by road or by cable car. The road experience is a bit time-consuming and laborious, but it is definitely enjoyable! The feeling of being on the mountain and watching the panoramic view of Tay Ninh land in sight is truly wonderful.
• However, according to the experience of many people, the most fun is definitely when you have to try the feeling of camping overnight here. Do not forget to prepare a little food for the party to organize an outdoor barbecue at the height of 900m. If you are looking for a day out in Saigon, please come here!

•Chua Chan Mountain is 800m high, second only to the majestic Ba Den mountain. Because it is not so developed as Ba Den mountain, Chua Chan mountain still retains a wild beauty. You can come here by motorbike then park your car below and start your journey to conquer by road.

• After conquering the top of the mountain, in front of your eyes at this moment is a beautiful scene created by rolling clouds that erase the whole sky. "
